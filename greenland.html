格陵兰岛：数据之城


2020 Spring UCLA AUD Suprastudio Technology Core Studio

"Deep Urbanism: Technocritical Architectural Narratives for Human/ Machine Society"

Design & Research Project


Groupmates: Xie Wei, Zhang Xiaoman

Supervisors:Ozel Guvenc, Benjamin Ennemoser

Tools:Pix2pix&CycleGAN, Rhino, Unreal Engine 4, Unity, AR&VR, Adobe After Effect


In the context of climate change, Greenland's ice sheet is melting rapidly, revealing vast new territories. This project explores the possibility of establishing a data center city on Greenland, leveraging its unique geographical advantages for sustainable computing infrastructure.
